Welcome

Sri Chennamaneni Rajeshwara Rao, a freedom fighter, is one of the veteran leaders in communist party of India with outstanding public life. Sri Chennamaneni Rajeshwara Rao is one among such selfless, spotless political leaders. He was born on 31st august 1923 in Manwada village of Boinapally mandal in Karimnagar

Rajeshwara Rao has participated in freedom struggle and was member of AP legislative assembly for 6 times starting from the year 1957. He was member of legislative assembly for 6 times starting from the year 1957. He won as M.L.A from choppadandi constituency (Karimnagar district) in 1957. He has won 5 times as M.L.A from Sircilla constituency in 1967, 1978, 1985, 1994 & 2004.

This website is all about his achievements, articles (political, economic), Autobiography, videos on various issues and about his family.
