Sri Ch. Rajeshwara Rao & Family

Sri Ch. Rajeshwara Rao garu is the eldest son of Sri Chennamaneni Srinivasa Rao and Smt. Chennamaneni Chandramma. Srinivas Rao garu was an agriculturist hailing from Nagaram village in Konaraopeta mandal of Karimnagar district. Rajeshwara Rao has three younger brothers and seven sisters. Among them the first younger brother is Ch.Hanuantha Rao garu (Freedom fighter, former Planning commission member, Govt of India). Next Ch.Venkateshwara Rao garu (former CPI leader Karimnagar District). Last younger brother is Ch.Vidyasagar Rao garu (former Union Minister of State for Home, Govt of India, BJP Leader).

Sri Ch.Rajeshwara Rao garu, married to Smt.Lalitha Devi (Freedom Fighter), eldest daughter of Sri Gourineni Ram Gopal Rao and Smt. Mangamma garu who hails from vemulawada town.

Rajeshwara Rao is blessed with 3 daughters and 1 son. Eldest daughter Smt Ch.Aurna is a retired High school teacher from Govt High School, married to Joginipally Rajeshwara Rao, former sarpanch of Narsingapuram village in boinipally mandal of Karimnagar district. Second daughter Smt Kalpana is a house wife married to Sri Vala Ram Mohan Rao, Retd. SE, AP Transco, Govt of A.P. Third daughter Smt Dr.Ch.Prabhavathi, Retired Additional Director AYUSH Department, Govt of India, She is married to Gourineni Sudhakar Rao, Contractor by profession.

Rajeshwara Rao’'s only son Dr.Ramesh Chennamaneni, is a Professor in Houmblt University, Berlin, Germany. Presently he is into active politics in AP, serving as sitting MLA for vemulawada constituency.

Rajeshwara Rao garu has 9 grandchildren and 7 great grandchildren. He love to spend his free time enjoying with kids and other family members. Smt Lalitha Devi dedicated her services, love to the family members.
